About Me

Hi, I am Alvaro Gonzalez Reynaud. I am currently a student at Binghamton University pursuing a degree in Graphic Design, Music, and a minor in Japanese.

I am originally from Mexico City but I moved to New York in in 2016 to pursue a higher education. My studies began with the hopes of becoming an animator but they morphed into wanting to become a Motion Graphic Designer.

With this web journal I am hoping to learn how to code a website that I can use as my personal portfolio and open the door to becoming a web designer.
